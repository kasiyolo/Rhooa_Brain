Your chatbot knows nothing about you

You ask about your client, your contract, last year's report — and get generic answers, because the model has no idea about your data.

With the Brain: your assistant pulls knowledge from your documents, emails, and projects the moment it needs it — and answers like a colleague who's worked at your company for years.

You can't upload company files to someone else's cloud

Contracts, financials, customer data on third-party servers? For most companies that's a non-starter — legally and practically.

With the Brain: your knowledge graph lives only on your PC, encrypted with AES-256. Only the minimum snippets needed per question ever transit — filtered, never stored server-side, with one-click full erasure (GDPR).
